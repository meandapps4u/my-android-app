# Privacy Policy - Trading Psychology Kit

**Last Updated: November 14, 2025**

## Introduction

Trading Psychology Kit ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device model, OS version, unique device identifier
- **Usage Analytics**: Features used, time spent, session duration (via Firebase Analytics)
- **Crash Reports**: Error logs and crash data (via Firebase Crashlytics)
- **Location**: Only if you explicitly grant permission

### Information You Provide
- **Trade Data**: Symbols, entry/exit prices, dates, and P&L information
- **Trading Rules**: Rules you create and save
- **Emotion Logs**: Emotional states and notes you record
- **Meditation Data**: Duration and timestamps of meditation sessions

All data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and local storage.

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Diagnose and fix technical issues
- Monitor app performance and analytics
- Send important updates about the app

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Your trading data, rules, and logs are stored locally on your device
- **Cloud Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics are sent to Firebase
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not sell or share your personal trading data with third parties

## Firebase Services

We use the following Firebase services:
- **Firebase Analytics**: To understand how you use the app
- **Firebase Crashlytics**: To detect and fix app crashes
- **Google Play Services**: For app distribution and updates

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Access your stored data anytime through the app
- Delete your data by clearing the app storage
- Opt-out of analytics in your device settings

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
